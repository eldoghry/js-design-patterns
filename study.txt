What Design patterns ? 
Design patterns are advanced object-oriented solutions to commonly occurring software problems. Patterns are about reusable designs and interactions of objects.

We can classiified design patterns to 3 diffenert Categories 

1- Creational Category
    - Factory pattern
    - Singlton Pattern: limit # of instance of object to be 0 or 1 
2- Structual Category
3- Behaivor Category

- Strategy Pattern: swap Strategies easily
- Iterator Pattern: used when I want to iterate collection
- Observer Pattern: one to many The Observer pattern offers a subscription model in which objects subscribe to an event and get notified when the event occurs.
